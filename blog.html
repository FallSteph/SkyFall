Intramurals

Last December 4, the BukSU Intramurals started with a big parade followed by the opening program symbolizing the start of different events. It was unforgettable because we were like adventurers finding different events that has a COT representatives in order to complete our pass-by-me cards.

1st General Assembly

On November 15, the COT 1st General Assembly was held with the theme of Hinahanap-Hanap Kita: A Journey Through the Filipino Retro Era.

Acquaintance Party

On the evening of November 15, The 1st Pakikipaghalubilo started at 6pm and ended between 8pm and 9pm. It was memorable because I enjoyed the event itself and I was also been mistook as a boy, which is kind of funny.
